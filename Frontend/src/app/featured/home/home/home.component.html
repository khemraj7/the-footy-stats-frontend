  What type of football stats do you offer ?

We offer a multitude of stats for over 1000 competitions worldwide. The list of football stats we cover are :

  • H2H Stats for Upcoming Fixtures
  • BTTS Stats
  • Corner Stats
  • Over 2.5 Goals
  • Expected Goals Stats
  • Football Livescores
  • Football League Stats

Aside from this list, we offer stats for every aspect of the football game and the stats are being updated by the minute. We recently included xG (Expected Goals) for hundreds of leagues, including the English Premier League's xG. Under the hood, we have thousands of data-points all our supported leagues. We crunch the numbers and make them easy and intuitive for our users.
